Source and governance model

Live source checks are public

The scheduled workflow updates data/live-sources.json with reachability, final URL, status and raw price-like tokens where visible.

Manual review before publishing prices

Automated tokens can be wrong or promotional. A human should verify price, term, roaming and final checkout before changing manual deal entries.
