Keep your number

Assisted mobile switching for people who hate tech

Mob Deals cannot move your number for you yet, but it can make the official UK switching steps simple. Use the buttons below on the phone that has the number you want to keep.

Official UK process: text PAC to 65075 to keep your number, STAC to 75075 for a new number, or INFO to 85075 to check contract/exit-fee information. Codes are normally valid for 30 days. See Ofcom’s mobile switching guidance.

Plain-English switching checklist

  1. Use INFO first if you are unsure about exit fees.
  2. Choose a new plan and check coverage at your postcode.
  3. Text PAC to 65075 from the old SIM if you want to keep your number.
  4. Give the PAC to the new provider during signup or in their account/app.
  5. Keep both SIMs handy on switch day. Some loss of service can happen while the number moves.
  6. Update banking, NHS, email and two-factor-authentication records if your number changes.

Read Ofcom’s switching guidance.
