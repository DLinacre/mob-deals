UK mobile provider and source directory

This directory is intentionally broad: core UK networks, MVNOs, supermarket/retail brands, comparison sources, specialist providers, business mobile services, travel eSIMs and legacy watchlist entries. It helps users decide what is suitable — and what to avoid — before signing a contract.

Decision rule: use core UK networks and UK MVNOs for normal number-porting contracts. Treat retailers, business mobile, eSIM/data-only and legacy entries as decision sources, not direct like-for-like replacements.
