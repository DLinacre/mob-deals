Methodology

Mob Deals compares SIM-only plans using structured fields: provider, underlying network, monthly price, data allowance, contract term, roaming note and provider/source URL.

What is verified?

Provider/source URLs are public links for users to check final details. The current static site does not claim real-time scraping, live lowest-price status or guaranteed availability.

What must be checked before switching

  • Final monthly price and any introductory period.
  • Contract length, cancellation terms and annual price-rise policy.
  • Fair-use, tethering and roaming allowance.
  • Coverage at your exact address using Ofcom and provider tools.

Future automation requirements

Any future scraper or API should store source URL, captured timestamp, old/new values, validation result and human approval before publishing.
